About Brilliant Tortoise Bubble Tea

Our Story

Brilliant Tortoise started with a simple question: Could slow-brewed tea still serve a campus rush? Turns out, yes. We prep leaves with care, then pour fast so you're back to class with a great sip.

Small by design, we focus on just a short menu of drinks we love. Look for the honey & yellow logo and the BT shell stamp on every cup.
